Introduction to UDSA

Being prepared can reduce fear, anxiety, and losses that accompany disasters.

Disaster management aims to reduce, or avoid, the potential losses from hazards, assure prompt and appropriate assistance to victims of disaster, and achieve rapid and effective recovery.

Most apps are educational, some are only limited to a specific region others only concerned with a particular calamity. We are developing an online database that can be accessed through an app or a website. Effective yet precise and user friendly.

Goals

  • Encouraging culture of disaster preparedness.
  • Institutionalisation of disaster management.
  • Instant response, Safety and effective decision making.
  • Post disaster medical help and first aid.
  • Providing financial independency
